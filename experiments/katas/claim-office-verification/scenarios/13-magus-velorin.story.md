# Magus Velorin

Velorin, the Hexenmeister vom Ostmoor, has been with the MHPCO for
just over a year. He insures his arcane working set first: an oak
staff, a silver amulet attuned to scrying, and three matched runes
he uses for warding circles.

Some weeks later, anticipating a long expedition, he takes out a
second policy on a shipment of seven additional runes — more than
he can fit into a single warding circle, but he likes to be
prepared.

A library cat sends his staff clattering to the floor; not long
after, an over-ambitious counterspell backfires through his amulet.

## Rules touched

- Stage 1: block of 3 alike components, high-enchantment surcharge,
  first insurance, deductible per damage event
- Stage 2: multi-item policy mixing main items and components,
  multiple policies under one customer, follow-up contract discount,
  block discount applied to premium but not insurance value
