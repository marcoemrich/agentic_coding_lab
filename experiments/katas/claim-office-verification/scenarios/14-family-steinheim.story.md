# Familie Steinheim

The Steinheim brothers — three soldiers, all sons of Aldric — pool
their resources for a single family policy. They register their
three battle-worn swords and their grandmother's protective amulet.

They are new to the MHPCO; the surveyor is patient with them.

Some months later, fire takes part of the family castle. Two of the
three swords come out warped and pitted; the third was hanging in a
hall the fire never reached, and the amulet was on the youngest
brother who was at market.

## Rules touched

- Stage 1: deductible per damage event
- Stage 2: multiple items of the same type in a single policy, claim
  damages a strict subset of insured items
