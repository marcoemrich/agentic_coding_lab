# Pechvogel Tordan

Tordan has been with the MHPCO for four years and is, by all
accounts, the unluckiest insured person in the office's records. He
keeps it simple: one sword, one healing potion. No curses, no
exotic enchantments, just bad timing.

In quick succession he stumbles down a staircase (sword damaged),
spills his potion in a tavern brawl, falls again on the way home
(sword again, worse this time), and finally manages a fourth tumble
that pushes the policy's cap from a comfortable buffer to flat zero.

## Rules touched

- Stage 1: deductible per damage event
- Stage 2: cap exhaustion across many successive claims, loyalty
  discount, first insurance, mixed item types
