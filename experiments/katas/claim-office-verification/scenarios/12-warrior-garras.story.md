# Krieger Garras

Garras the Berserker has been with the MHPCO for five years. He
insures his combat gear: a cursed sword (mid-enchanted, of course),
a silver protection amulet, and a healing potion. A few weeks later
he summons a fresh dragon-bone staff and takes out a second policy
on it.

A red dragon eventually catches him on the road north. The attack
mauls his sword and dents the amulet, but the staff and potion
survive. Later, in a tavern, his staff backfires during a clumsy
spell and takes some damage too.

## Rules touched

- Stage 1: cursed surcharge, high-enchantment surcharge, loyalty
  discount, first insurance, deductible per damage event
- Stage 2: multi-item policy, multiple policies under one customer,
  follow-up contract discount, dragon material on a low-enchantment
  item, modifier scope on multi-item policies
